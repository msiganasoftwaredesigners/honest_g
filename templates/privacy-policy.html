Privacy Policy of Honest G

Last Updated: June 2025

Thank you for visiting samiahomes.com, the official website of Honest G Real Estate, based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website and real estate services.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information: When you contact us, submit inquiries, or register interest in a property, we may collect personal information such as your full name, email address, phone number, and preferences regarding property types or budget.

B. Property Inquiry Details: If you request property information or schedule a visit, we may collect related details like preferred location, budget range, or additional comments.

C. Usage Data: We may collect information about your device and browsing behavior, including your IP address, browser type, device type, referral source, and visited pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

A. Property Recommendations: To provide tailored property listings based on your interests or submitted inquiries.

B. Communication: To respond to your inquiries, confirm appointment requests, or share updates on available properties and new developments.

C. Service Improvement: To analyze user activity and optimize our website and customer experience.

D. Marketing: With your consent, we may send you newsletters or updates about new property launches or investment opportunities. You can opt out at any time.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it under these limited circumstances:

A. Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist us in delivering services (e.g., CRM systems or marketing tools) under confidentiality agreements.

B. Legal Obligations: If required by law or to comply with legal processes.

4. Your Choices

A. Access & Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us.

B. Marketing Preferences: You can opt out of receiving promotional messages at any time by following the unsubscribe link or contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to be cautious when sharing personal information online.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page, with an updated "Last Updated" date.
